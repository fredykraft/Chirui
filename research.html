Research

My research focuses on quantitative finance with specialization in operations, information systems, and technology. I explore how technology, data, and information management drive financial and operational excellence.

Research Focus Areas

  • Operations & Finance: Process optimization, operational risk, efficiency analysis
  • Information Systems: Data management, system architecture, information technology in finance
  • Financial Technology: Algorithmic approaches, computational methods, tech-enabled finance
  • Quantitative Analysis: Mathematical modeling, statistical inference, data analytics
